Daphnie Insights Music Distribution Platform

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026 | Effective date: May 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Daphnie Insights ("we", "our", or "the Platform") collects, uses, stores, and protects information obtained through your use of our music content distribution platform and its integration with the TikTok API.

By using Daphnie Insights, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account to Daphnie Insights, we collect the following information through the TikTok API:

  • Basic account information — via the user.info.basic scope: your TikTok user ID and display name, used solely to identify and authenticate your account on our platform.
  • Content publishing data — via the video.publish scope: metadata associated with video uploads, including publish status and upload identifiers, used solely to confirm successful delivery of your content to TikTok.

We do not collect passwords, financial information, private messages, follower lists, or any data beyond what is necessary to provide the content scheduling and publishing service.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected through the TikTok API is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your identity and link your TikTok account to the platform
  • To publish video content to your TikTok account at scheduled times on your behalf
  • To verify successful publication and update your content schedule accordingly
  • To maintain the security and integrity of the platform

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose other than providing the content distribution service you have requested.

3. Data Storage and Security

OAuth access tokens obtained through TikTok authentication are stored locally on your device in an encrypted configuration file. These tokens are not transmitted to or stored on any third-party server operated by us.

Video files are processed locally on your device and temporarily uploaded to a secure cloud storage service (Dropbox) for the sole purpose of transferring them to TikTok. Files are deleted from temporary storage immediately after upload is confirmed.

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data, including encrypted storage of credentials and secure HTTPS communication with all third-party APIs.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except as described below:

  • TikTok — video content and associated metadata are transmitted to TikTok via their Content Posting API as part of the core service. TikTok's own Privacy Policy governs their handling of this data: tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Dropbox — used as a temporary transfer intermediary to provide TikTok with a publicly accessible video URL. Files are not retained. Dropbox's Privacy Policy applies: dropbox.com/privacy

We do not share your TikTok account information, user ID, or any other personal data with any other third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide the service:

  • OAuth tokens are retained on your device until you disconnect your account or revoke access
  • Video files are deleted from temporary storage immediately after successful upload to TikTok
  • Schedule and caption data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by you at any time
  • Upload logs are retained for up to 30 days for debugging purposes and then deleted automatically

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access — you may request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Deletion — you may request deletion of your data at any time by disconnecting your TikTok account from the platform
  • Revocation — you may revoke the platform's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's app settings under Settings → Security → Authorised Apps
  • Portability — schedule and caption data is stored in standard CSV format on your device and can be exported or deleted directly

7. Children's Privacy

Daphnie Insights is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including countries where TikTok and Dropbox operate their servers. By using the platform, you consent to such transfers. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any such transfers in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the platform after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
